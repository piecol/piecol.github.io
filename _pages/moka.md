MOKA is a compact carrier board for Lattepanda MU, x86 single-board computer.

![MOKA_FRONT](/assets/IMG/MOKA/moka_front.png)

## Highlights

- Gigabit ethernet
- USB-PD @ 12V
- HDMI
- USB2.0 2X
- USB3.0
- M-Key M.2 PCIe 2230/42
- E-Key M.2 PCIe 2230/42
- CSI camera
- Debug UART
- QWIIK I2C
- FAN
