I am a hardware developer focused on embedded systems, PCB design, rapid prototyping, and open-source product development.

My work is centered on turning ideas into robust, manufacturable devices, with particular attention to compact system architecture, integration constraints, and practical deployment.

I work across:
- carrier boards and embedded electronics
- prototype-to-product development
- mechanical/electronic integration
- open-source hardware workflows

Over time, I have developed projects ranging from compact compute-module carrier boards to custom embedded platforms for real-world applications.

## Approach

I like to build in public, validate early, and use open development as a way to improve both design quality and collaboration.

## Selected projects

- **CM5 Minima** — compact Raspberry Pi CM5 carrier board
- **MOKA** — embedded hardware platform focused on integration and enclosure-ready design
